Kaley Brauer
Astrophysics Ph.D.,  Massachusetts Institute of Technology


Computational Astrophysicist


I study galaxy formation and the astrophysical origins of heavy elements using cutting-edge simulations. My specialty is the smallest, earliest galaxies that merged into the Milky Way throughout cosmic time.

In April 2023, I defended my Physics Ph.D. at MIT. In July 2023, I will join Harvard University as an NSF Postdoctoral Fellow!

When I am not doing research, I enjoy science outreach, dancing, art, hiking, and bicycling.
